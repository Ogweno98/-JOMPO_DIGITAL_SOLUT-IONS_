Green Energy Deployment

Installation of solar panels (100W–600W) to power rural homes, farms, schools, and health centers. We've empowered over 300 households with clean, affordable energy.

Smart Agriculture Training

We conduct IoT-enabled training and digital literacy programs for farmers, integrating climate-smart techniques with data-driven tools for crop production.

Borehole Drilling for Water Access

We drill and maintain community boreholes to ensure sustainable water access in arid and semi-arid areas across Kenya.

Tree Planting & Biodiversity

As part of our environmental initiative, we’ve planted over 10,000 trees and restored degraded landscapes through community mobilization.

Dream Soccer Talent Empowerment

This flagship program empowers youth through football, mentorship, and digital education—bridging talent with opportunity and promoting positive change.
